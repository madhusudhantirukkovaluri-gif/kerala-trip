Kerala: Kozhikode & Wayanad – 7 Days Trip

Affordable, full-experience trip with nature, heritage, and adventure

Day 1 – Arrival in Kozhikode (Calicut)

Morning: Arrive at Kozhikode Railway Station → Check-in at a centrally-located mid-range hotel.

Afternoon: Relax → Head to Kozhikode Beach for sea breeze and street snacks → Visit Pazhassi Raja Museum.

Evening: Walk through SM Street (Mittai Theruvu) for shopping and local treats → Dinner at a budget-friendly Kerala restaurant.

Day 2 – Kozhikode → Wayanad: Arrival & Caves

Morning: Quick breakfast → Take KSRTC bus/shared cab to Wayanad (3-4 hours). Check-in at a cozy homestay or budget hotel in Sultan Bathery or Kalpetta.

Afternoon: Edakkal Caves trek → Stop at Phantom Rock.

Evening: Dinner at a local eatery; relax at your stay.

Day 3 – Chembra Peak Trek & Waterfalls

Morning: Early morning head to Chembra Peak (permit + guide, pack snacks/water).

Afternoon: Cool off at Soochipara Waterfalls.

Evening: Local dinner → Rest at your hotel/homestay.

Day 4 – Wildlife Safari & Tribal Culture

Morning: Early jeep safari at Tholpetty Wildlife Sanctuary.

Afternoon: Visit En Ooru Tribal Village for a culture walk → Lunch at a nearby dhaba.

Evening: Dinner at a local spot.

Day 5 – Islands, Lakes, and Boating

Morning: Visit Kuruvadweep (entry + bamboo rafting).

Afternoon: Stroll around or boat at Pookode Lake.

Evening: Tea at a local café, dinner at homestay.

Day 6 – Dam, Temples, and Scenic Wayanad

Morning: Head to Banasura Sagar Dam (entry, optional boating).

Afternoon: Visit Sree Thirunelli Mahavishnu Temple, picnic by Kalindi River.

Evening: Relax and pack for departure.

Day 7 – Return to Kozhikode & Departure

Morning: Return by bus/cab to Kozhikode.

Afternoon: Last-minute shopping at SM Street or visit Pazhassi Raja Museum if missed on Day 1.

Evening: Head to Kozhikode Railway Station for your train to Hyderabad.
